About Wonwoo

Wonwoo (born June 8, 1996) is a South Korean rapper, singer, and songwriter. He is a member of the K-pop group Seventeen.

Career and Achievements

Wonwoo made his debut as a member of Seventeen in 2015. Since then, the group has released several successful albums and won multiple awards.

Fun Facts

  • Wonwoo is a talented rapper and has been praised for his flow and delivery.
  • He is known for his sense of humor and frequently makes his members and fans laugh.
  • Wonwoo has a cat named Kkum-ee.
